Celebration Time

Every day from 9:00pm - 9:15pm we will launch fireworks over the park. There will be no fireworks launch on July 4th as there is already one planned at 8:00pm.
On Christmas Eve and Day fireworks launch from 9:00pm - 9:20pm. Once celebration time starts most rides and restaurants will close.

Tampering with or interfering with the fireworks in any way will result in a fine of over $250 and being banned from all of the park locations.
