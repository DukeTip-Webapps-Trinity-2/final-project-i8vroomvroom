Q:Are we allowed to bring food and drinks inside the park?

A: As long as the food and drinks do not have alcahol, you are fine.


Q: How much do the tickets cost?

A: They are 12$ for kids 12 and younger, 18$ for adults, and 10$ for veterans 60 years or older.


Q: How many rides do you have?

A: We have about 50 attractions in our park ready to be ridden on and enjoyed!


Q: What is your schedule and what days are you open?

A: We are open everyday from 9am to 11pm!
