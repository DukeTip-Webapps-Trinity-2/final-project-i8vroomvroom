AmuseMe Rides




Warning: Extreme rides suitable for only healthy folks.


Sky Horror

Good luck on this one! There is no floor on the car, so you'll just be dangling while doing a barrel roll!

Reversing Twister

Be prepared for a suprise! Instead of going forwards, you go backwards! You'll have no clue what hit you!

Mad Physics

Watch Out! This ride will do all sorts of wacky (but safe) things to your body. Prepare to fix your hair after this!

Rusher

This coaster will make you scream! This coaster shall suprise you with sudden accelerations and dives!

Strafe Up

Scared of heights? Don't go on this ride! This coaster is the tallest coaster boasting at 375 feet tall! Don't worry, this coaster is a lot slower than most. Oh yeah, and also be prepred to dangle.

Mach Wild

The fastest coaster in the park, you're in for a blurry ride!
